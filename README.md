  ## Formula 1 Performance Analytics

An end-to-end performance analytics project that applies relational database design, SQL, and business intelligence techniques to evaluate Formula One team performance. Inspired by the analytical culture of Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS Formula One Team, the project demonstrates how data can support strategic decision-making aimed at improving championship competitiveness.

Modern Formula One teams rely extensively on data to optimise performance, inform race strategy, and guide long-term technical development. This project simulates the role of a Performance or Business Analyst by examining historical race data to understand the factors that influence championship success. Using publicly available race data, the project develops a structured analytical framework to evaluate driver, constructor, circuit, and race performance, with the objective of identifying opportunities that could improve competitive performance in future seasons.

Project Objective:

To demonstrate how data analytics supports strategic, operational, and performance decision-making within a Formula One team by transforming historical race data into actionable insights.

Business Scenario:

Following the conclusion of the 2024 Formula One season, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS aims to strengthen its challenge for both the Drivers’ and Constructors’ Championships. Acting as a Performance Analyst, the objective is to analyse historical race and qualifying data to identify competitive strengths, performance gaps, and strategic opportunities that could improve results in the following season.

Business Questions:

1. Which factors most strongly influence championship success?
2. How has team performance evolved across multiple seasons?
3. How consistent are drivers throughout a season?
4. Does qualifying position strongly predict race results?
5. Which rival teams gained the greatest competitive advantage?
6. Which circuits favour our car and which expose weaknesses?
7. Which drivers consistently outperform expectations?
8. Which circuits should receive greater strategic preparation?

Project Limitations:

This project is based on publicly available Formula One data and therefore does not include telemetry, tyre degradation, fuel strategy, aerodynamic performance, or detailed pit stop telemetry. The analysis focuses on the data typically available after each race weekend, allowing strategic and performance insights to be generated despite these constraints. Consequently, analyses relating to race engineering, tyre strategy optimisation, fuel management, and driver telemetry fall outside the scope of this project.

Highlights

• Relational Database Design
• Data Modelling
• SQL Query Development
• Star Schema Design
• KPI Development
• Tableau Dashboards
• Business Performance Analysis
• Statistical Trend Analysis
• Data Cleaning & Validation

Expected Business Impact

Although based on publicly available race data, the analytical framework demonstrates how performance insights can support evidence-based decision-making within a Formula One team. The outputs can help identify competitive strengths, monitor key performance indicators, evaluate driver and constructor consistency, and inform strategic planning for future seasons.

  ## Swiftfulfill Project

An end-to-end operations analytics project designed to demonstrate how data can support inventory management, order fulfilment, customer analysis, and commercial decision-making within a logistics and fulfilment environment.

Modern fulfilment businesses generate large volumes of operational data across customers, orders, inventory, shipments, suppliers, and employees. Transforming this data into meaningful insights enables organisations to improve operational efficiency, reduce costs, enhance customer experience, and make evidence-based business decisions.

This project simulates the role of a Business Analyst supporting a fulfilment operation by designing a relational database and analysing business performance using SQL, Python and Excel.

Project Objective:

To demonstrate how operational data can be transformed into actionable business insights that support decision-making across sales performance, customer behaviour, inventory management, supplier performance, and fulfilment operations.

Business Scenario:

SwiftFulfil is experiencing continued business growth, resulting in increased order volumes and operational complexity. Senior management requires greater visibility into operational performance to support strategic planning and day-to-day decision-making.

Acting as a Business Analyst, the objective is to consolidate operational data into a structured analytical model that enables stakeholders to monitor performance, identify inefficiencies, and uncover opportunities for improvement.

Business Questions:

1. Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
2. Which customer segments contribute most to sales?
3. How have revenue and order volumes changed over time?
4. Which cities or regions generate the strongest commercial performance?
5. Which suppliers support the highest-performing product categories?
6. Which products should be prioritised based on sales performance?
7. How effectively are customer orders being fulfilled?
8. What operational trends should management monitor to improve efficiency?

Project Limitations:

This project is based on a simulated operational dataset and therefore does not include real-time warehouse operations, inventory movement, workforce productivity, or transportation tracking. During data validation, inconsistencies were identified within shipment dates, resulting in negative fulfilment durations for a number of records. These anomalies were documented and excluded from fulfilment time analysis to preserve analytical integrity.

Technical Scope

•	Relational Database Design
•	SQL Query Development
•	ETL & Data Preparation
•	Data Cleaning & Validation
•	Customer Segmentation
•	Sales & Revenue Analysis
•	Operational KPI Development
•	Business Performance Reporting

Business Impact

The project demonstrates how business intelligence can provide operational visibility across the fulfilment process. By consolidating data from multiple business functions into a single analytical framework, stakeholders can monitor key performance indicators, identify growth opportunities, improve operational efficiency, and support evidence-based decision-making.
